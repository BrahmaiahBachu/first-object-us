Unlock the Power of BOT Model

Empower your organization with First Object’s BOT (Build, Operate & Transform) model, designed to deliver custom IT talent solutions that grow with your business.

How BOT Works

We provide a seamless, efficient process tailored to your needs.

Consultation & Needs Assessment

We begin by thoroughly understanding your business needs, company culture, and job requirements. Our team works closely with you to create a clear and targeted job description that attracts top-tier talent.

Candidate Search & Screening

Using our extensive network and cutting-edge recruitment tools, we source candidates who align with your needs. We perform in-depth screenings, including interviews, skill assessments, and background.

Candidate Presentation & Interview Coordination

We present a shortlist of qualified candidates to you, handling the logistics of scheduling and coordinating interviews. Our team helps facilitate communication to ensure a smooth interview.

Offer & Onboarding

Once you've selected the perfect candidate, we assist in extending a formal offer, and handling the onboarding process. This ensures a seamless transition and a successful start your new employee.

Why Choose BOT?
  • 01.Build
    • Identify and recruit the right talent.
    • Set up a dedicated team tailored to your business needs.
    • Establish robust infrastructure and processes.
  • 02.Operate

    • Ensure seamless execution of projects and deliverables.
    • Maintain quality and productivity through continuous monitoring.
  • 03.Transform
    • Gradual transfer of operations to your organization.
    • Train and integrate the team with your in-house processes.
    • Enable long-term growth and sustainability.
